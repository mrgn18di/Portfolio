About Me

Hi, I’m Morgan, an Interaction Design student interested in how design can bridge communication between people and ideas. I like exploring different mediums, from motion graphics to information design, to create work that is functional, expressive, and easy to understand.

I believe design should be clear, respectful, and intentional. My goal isn’t to make things look complicated, but to make them feel effortless. Whether it’s a visual narrative or an interactive experience, I try to design with empathy so people can connect with a message without needing an explanation.

Good design, to me, lives in the small details: hierarchy, rhythm, timing, and tone. I value work that feels calm, honest, and thoughtfully crafted.
