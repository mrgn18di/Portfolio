Exploring how motion and interaction can turn ideas into stories people feel.


I’m Morgan Danendra Inderadjajanata, an Interaction Design student who explores how visuals, motion, and interaction can communicate ideas simply and beautifully. My work blends design thinking with storytelling, from transforming a Google logo into a national celebration to turning a product manual into an engaging visual experience.
