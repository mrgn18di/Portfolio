Braun M90 – Instructional Infographic

Overview

The Braun M90 infographic reimagines a traditional product manual into a clean, visual storytelling piece. Inspired by Braun’s design philosophy of “less but better,” it illustrates how to use and maintain the shaver through minimal text, clear hierarchy, and modern iconography. Designed in Illustrator, it combines precision and simplicity, turning instructions into an engaging visual experience that aligns with Braun’s timeless approach to functional design.
